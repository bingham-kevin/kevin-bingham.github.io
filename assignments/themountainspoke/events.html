Cycle the Continental Divide

June 22 - July 01, 2018

Spend a week with us as we climb up and over the Rocky Mountains. Start in Bozeman and end in Idaho Falls. We travel 250 miles and climb over 6000 feet in elevation. We will cross the Continental Divide in Caribou-Targhee National Forest.

  • Recommended for intermediate and experienced riders
  • Meals and water provided
  • Scenic route through Yellowstone
  • Guides are trained EMTs
  • Cost: $673 per rider
  • Private tours available for groups.
  • Check our contact page to inquire.
